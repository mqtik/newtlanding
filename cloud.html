Limitless
storage

Unlimited storage for all users, in your device and the cloud. This will allow you to have multiple devices in sync, and not lose a word if you've lost your phone.

Revision
History

Every time you save, a new revision is created; so you can have versions of your own work across time.

You can even set an old revision as the winning one, or merge multiple revisions into one. Your followers will get a notification if you create a new chapter, so they can stay tuned to your work.

Conflicts?

This is why Newt has been designed this way; to open doors between you and potential collaborators.
This comes in handy too if you changed something from your computer and there are changes in your phone out of sync. We automate most of the conflicts so you don't have to even think about it.

But we leave the door open in case you're wanting to handle the resolution-conflict yourself.

Collaborate

Editing can be an daunting task for writers. Especially when they are begining.
Sometimes you'll find yourself modifying a part over and over, until is perfect.
What if there was a way to bring the people you want to help you build it?

Since you can handle the revisions of your work across time in the way that you want, you can invite anyone to read your book. Every living person has their own perspective.
Opinions exist almost since the beginning of time.
But what if they can edit the exact part that makes them feel that way, and send it to you for approval or rejection?
And if you like it, you can merge it into your book with just one touch!

Storage

Store unlimited content, media and more, and keep your work safe across devices, with revisins across time.

Sync

Seameless replication between devices. Just keep working offline, Changes you've made in your device will be replicated to the cloud whenever possible.

Secure

When you create drafts, it's private and encrypted by default. You can choose not to use the cloud, and keep all your work only in your device.

Publishers

While we're focused on writers-first, we feel it's totally necessary to give you the chance to publish it for everyone, and that you can read other people's work.

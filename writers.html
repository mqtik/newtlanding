We encourage you to publish in Newt! There are lots of resources for writers.
Setting the book to status Public, means that it will be available for everyone in the world to discover. It also means that the rights of the book are yours and yours only. We only stand as a bridge.
Newt it's a secure way to reach your followers and wider audiences you wouldn't normally get to meet.

They will be able to interact with your book, posting comments between lines and chapters, highlight its best parts, spread the world for it. In the near future, we will provide a way (only if you allow it) for them to send you private reviews and contributions for your work.

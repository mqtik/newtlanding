More for writers

Thunder
Cloud

There are words floating through the air!
They're all over the place.
Dancing between the cloud and your devices.

Two-Way
Sync

Unlimited storage for all users, in your device and the cloud. This will allow you to have multiple devices in sync, and not lose a word if you lost your phone.

Revision
History

Every time you save, a new revision is created; so you can have versions of your own work across time.

You can even set an old revision as the winning one, or merge multiple revisions into one. Your followers will get a notification if you create a new chapter, so they can stay tuned to your work.

Conflicts?

This is why Newt has been designed this way; to open doors betweenn you and potential collaborators.
This comes in handy too if you changed something from your computer and there are changes in your phone out of sync. We automate most of the conflicts so you don't have to even think about it.

But we leave the door open in case you're wanting to handle the resolution-conflict yourself.
